## 1. Official Google Courses

### üåü Recommended Starting Point

If you're just starting your journey, it's highly recommended to begin with **[Android Basics with Compose](https://developer.android.com/courses/android-basics-compose/course)**. This is the best course to start with because it's created by Google and completely free. No prior knowledge of Kotlin or Android development is required ‚Äî the Google team will guide you through everything step-by-step with video lessons and interactive codelabs using **Jetpack Compose**, the modern toolkit for building Android UIs.

## 2. YouTube Channels
* **[Android Developers](https://www.youtube.com/c/AndroidDevelopers)** ‚Äî the official channel from Google.

* **[Philipp Lackner](https://www.youtube.com/c/PhilippLackner)** ‚Äî high-quality tutorials on advanced Android topics such as Jetpack Compose, Clean Architecture, Dependency Injection, and testing. ## 3. Online Courses
* **[Google Play Academy ‚Äî Certificates & Training](https://playacademy.withgoogle.com/certificate/)** ‚Äî official free certification courses from Google to help you understand app growth, store listing optimization, monetization, policy compliance, and more.
* **[Coursera: UX Design by Google](https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-ux-design)** ‚Äî a specialized UX course from Google.
* **[Programming Fundamentals in Kotlin (Meta)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/meta-programming-fundamentals-kotlin)** ‚Äî a short introductory course from Meta that covers the basics of Kotlin and object-oriented programming. Ideal for those new to Kotlin.
* **[Advanced Programming in Kotlin (Meta)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/advanced-programming-in-kotlin)** ‚Äî a short course by Meta covering advanced Kotlin topics including functional programming, generics, and more. Useful for strengthening your Kotlin foundation.
* **[Udemy: Android Development Courses](https://www.udemy.com/courses/search/?src=ukw&q=Android+Development)** ‚Äî You can find a wide range of Android development courses on Udemy. Be sure to read the reviews and comments before purchasing, as some courses may be outdated or not clearly explained. However, there are also many high-quality courses available if you choose carefully.

## 6. Useful GitHub Repositories
